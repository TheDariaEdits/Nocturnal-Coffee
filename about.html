About Us

  • Coffee

    Oasis Cafe is a dedicated multi-roaster, supplying a rotating menu of coffees from some of the best roasters all across the country. In addition to brewed coffee and espresso drinks, we offer a selection of teas and smoothies.

  • Food

    At Oasis, all our bread and baked goods are made fresh on site every morning. Our food is fresh and simple. We have created a menu for vegans, pescatarians, and meat-lovers to enjoy without compromise.

  • Space

    The Oasis Cafe is dedicated to friendly service in a welcoming environment. We welcome all our customers to relax or bring a laptop to be productive in a cozy, calm atmosphere.
